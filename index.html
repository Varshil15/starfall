Server Rules

Please read and follow all rules to ensure a great experience for everyone

Playing on the Server

  • No hacking, cheating, or using unfair client modifications
  • No exploiting bugs or glitches for personal gain
  • No item duplication or use of duplication glitches
  • Respect other players' claims and property
  • Report any bugs or exploits to staff immediately
  • Follow all instructions given by staff members
  • Have fun and be respectful to all players!

In-Game Chat Rules

  • No spam, flooding, or excessive caps
  • No hate speech, racism, or discrimination
  • No harassment, bullying, or toxic behavior
  • Keep language respectful and family-friendly
  • No begging for items, ranks, or special treatment
  • No impersonating staff members or other players
  • Keep discussions appropriate and respectful

Discord Server Rules

  • Follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • No NSFW content or inappropriate material
  • No spam pinging users or staff members
  • Respect all staff members and their decisions
  • Use channels for their intended purposes
  • No excessive self-promotion or advertising
  • Keep voice channels civil and respectful
  • No mic spamming or soundboard abuse
  • Report issues to staff using proper channels
  • Be welcoming to new members of the community

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.

If you have questions about the rules, please contact a staff member.
